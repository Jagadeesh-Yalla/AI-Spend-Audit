# Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits at roughly 20–40% below retail.
A startup spending $2,000/month on AI tools switching to Credex credits:
- Annual spend: $24,000
- Credex margin (estimated 15–25%): $3,600–$6,000/year per customer
- Conservative LTV (2-year retention): **$7,200 per converted customer**

## CAC at Each GTM Channel

| Channel | Est. Visitors | Conversion to audit | Conversion to consultation | CAC |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Hacker News Show HN | 500 | 30% (150 audits) | 3% (4–5 bookings) | $0 |
| Reddit posts | 200 | 20% (40 audits) | 2% (1 booking) | $0 |
| LinkedIn DMs | 50 | 40% (20 audits) | 10% (2 bookings) | $0 |
| Twitter thread | 300 | 15% (45 audits) | 2% (1 booking) | $0 |

All channels above are $0 CAC. At scale, paid acquisition via Google ($15–25 CPC for "AI tool costs") would yield ~$500 CAC — still profitable given $7,200 LTV.

## Conversion Funnel Math

```
1,000 audit completions
→ 8% email capture = 80 leads
→ 25% of high-savings leads book consultation = ~10 consultations
→ 40% of consultations convert to credit purchase = 4 customers
→ $7,200 LTV × 4 = $28,800 revenue per 1,000 audits
→ Revenue per audit completed: $28.80
```

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

**What must be true:**

Month 1–3: Get to 500 audits/month via organic channels
Month 4–6: 2,000 audits/month — HN, Reddit, Twitter flywheel working
Month 7–12: 5,000 audits/month — SEO kicks in for "AI tool cost" queries
Month 13–18: 10,000 audits/month — embed widget in partner blogs

At 10,000 audits/month:
- 800 email leads/month (8% capture rate)
- 100 high-savings consultations/month
- 40 credit purchases/month at $7,200 LTV
- Monthly revenue contribution: $28,800/month → $345,600 ARR

To reach $1M ARR: need either 3× conversion rate improvement OR 3× audit volume.
**Most likely lever: improve email → consultation conversion from 25% to 50%** via better follow-up sequences targeting the specific tools the user was overpaying for.

## Key Assumptions

- Average startup AI spend audited: $800/month
- Average savings found: $340/month (42% reduction)
- High-savings threshold (>$500/mo): ~20% of all audits
- Credex margin: 20% of redirected spend
